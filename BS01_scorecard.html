Build the Stack | Where Each Tool Meets You
Two Layers of the Stack. Four Tools.
The accounting layer is correct and slow. The treasury layer is fast and approximate. Real corporates run both functions side by side. So should the in-between operator. The vertical axis on the accounting side is how much accounting discipline the tool requires from you to start. The treasury side is realtime and cloud-native by design.
Accounting Layer · Books of record · Daily and monthly
Treasury Layer · Realtime · Decisions today
Quicken
$96 / year · single user · 14k US/CA banks
Adapts to the operator. Cash or accrual. Single or double entry. Books and net worth in one login.
GL. Built in background as you categorize.
Subledger. Basic A/R and A/P. No aged buckets.
Reporting. P&L and Schedule C clean. Balance sheet loose.
Mgmt reporting. Business + personal + investments in one dashboard.
Banking. Mature since 1983. 87% match first pass.

Hides the accounting machinery behind a setup wizard. Path of least resistance for outside CPAs.

Reporting. US small business standard for 20 years.
Mgmt reporting. Class, location, project profitability.
Banking. Deepest integrations: Stripe, Shopify, payroll.

Xero
$540 / year · unlimited users · 21k banks

Banking. JAX AI auto-match 80%. Some feeds carry fees.
